# Wayrune — domain & trademark checklist (ops)

**Product:** Wayrune (WAY-roon)  
**Studio / company:** CodePoetry

## Domains (register immediately)

| Domain | Role |
|--------|------|
| **wayrune.com** | Canonical global |
| **wayrune.ai** | AI / product surface |
| **wayrune.in** | India / local market trust |

Actions:

- [ ] Register all three TLDs under the same registrant/org
- [ ] Lock DNS (registry lock / 2FA on registrar)
- [ ] Decide redirect policy: `.ai` and `.in` → `.com` (or country-specific landing) under one brand policy
- [ ] Do **not** announce the brand publicly until at least `.com` is owned (prefer all three)

## Trademark filing

### India (priority for Wayrune.in market)

- [ ] Clearance search on the Indian Trademark Registry
- [ ] File Classes **9** (software) and **42** (SaaS / IT services)
- [ ] Consider **35** / **39** / **43** if marketplace, booking, or hospitality services are sold under the mark
- [ ] Attorney review of proximity: Wayward, Waypoint, Wayru, and similar “Way*” travel marks

### International (if selling abroad)

- [ ] USPTO clearance + filing (Classes 9 & 42)
- [ ] EUIPO clearance + filing
- [ ] Madrid Protocol strategy once home filings are underway

## Launch readiness

Code may ship branded before registration completes. **Marketing launch** should wait for:

1. Domain ownership (all three preferred)
2. Trademark filing **started** (India at minimum)
3. Attorney note on sound-alike risk accepted
